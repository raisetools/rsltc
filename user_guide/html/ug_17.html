7 Showing module dependencies

These are shown in a simple ASCII representation. For example, generating them for the scheme QSI from section 10.14 produces the output 

QSI
        QSPEC
                QSP
        QUICKSORT
                QSP
        I

which shows that QSI depends on QSPEC, QUICKSORT and I, and that the first two of these depend in turn on QSP.
