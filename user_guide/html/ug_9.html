1.8 Features of RSL not supported

The tool follows The RAISE Development Method [2] in not allowing embedded schemes (schemes defined within classes). Schemes should be defined at the top level and put in their own files (see section 2).

The axiom quantification forall has been removed. Axioms should be quantified individually. This also follows The RAISE Development Method.

Chris George, April 17, 2008
